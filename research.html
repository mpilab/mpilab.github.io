Research

We develop methods that enable machines to perceive, understand, and generate across vision, language, and beyond.

Research Areas
✨

Diffusion Models

Diffusion-based generative frameworks for image synthesis, prompt inversion, novel view synthesis, and content personalization using low-rank adaptation techniques.

🔗

Vision–Language Models

Cross-modal alignment between visual and textual representations for visual story generation, diverse captioning, text-guided image manipulation, and multimodal LLM distillation.

🧠

Representation Learning

Structured latent spaces using normalizing flows, VAEs, and Wasserstein autoencoders to disentangle content, style, and domain-specific factors across modalities.

👁️

Semantic Correspondence

Unsupervised discovery of semantic keypoints and correspondences by exploiting rich internal representations from pretrained diffusion models—no labeled data needed.

⚡

Efficient Vision AI

Weight-trajectory hypernetworks and attention-guided alignment methods for compact, efficient vision-language models with reduced computational footprint.

🚗

Autonomous Systems

Distilling knowledge from large multimodal language models into lightweight vision-based end-to-end planners for real-world autonomous driving.

Our Principles
01

Theory before scale

We ground every method in rigorous probabilistic or geometric reasoning before scaling to practice.

02

Multimodal by design

Vision and language are complementary views of a shared perceptual reality, not separate silos.

03

Generalize, don't memorize

Models that truly understand generalize; models that memorize fail silently in the wild.
