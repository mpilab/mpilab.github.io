Join Us

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers passionate about computer vision, generative models, and multimodal AI. York University is in Toronto — Canada's AI hub and home of the Vector Institute.

🎓

PhD Students

  • Strong mathematical background and deep learning skills
  • Proficiency in Python / PyTorch
  • Prior research experience or publications is a plus
  • Fully funded positions available
📚

Master Students

  • Self-motivated with passion for research
  • Good programming and ML foundations
  • Commitment to thesis or project goals
  • MASc (thesis) and MEng (project) positions
🔬

Undergraduate Researchers

  • Curiosity-driven and fast learner
  • York University students preferred
  • Minimum 10 hrs/week for one semester
  • Academic credit or hourly compensation
How to Apply

PhD Students

Application Process
  1. Email with your CV and a short research statement
  2. Introductory call to assess initial fit
  3. Half-day interview: research presentation + 1-on-1 with lab members + research direction discussion
  4. Offer
  5. Application deadlines at York University are December 15
  6. Apply to York University Graduate Admissions listing Prof. Shweta Mahajan as supervisor

Posted: January 1, 2026 · Fall intake

Master Students (MASc / MEng)

Application Process
  1. Email with your CV, transcript, and a brief note on what you'd like to work on
  2. Introductory call
  3. Interview: short research presentation + discussion of future directions
  4. Offer and formal application to York University

Posted: January 1, 2026 · Rolling admissions

Undergraduate Research Assistants

Application Process
  1. Email with your CV, transcript, and a 1-paragraph description of your research interests
  2. Short introductory meeting
